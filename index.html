My Favorites Cast

Andrew Garfield

Actor

Andrew Russell Garfield (born 20 August 1983) is an English and American actor.Garfield gained wider recognition for playing Spider-Man in the superhero films The Amazing Spider-Man (2012), The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014), and later in Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021). He received nominations for the Academy Award for Best Actor for starring as Desmond Doss in the war film Hacksaw Ridge (2016) and as Jonathan Larson in the musical Tick, Tick Boom! (2021).

Chen ZheYuan

Actor

Chen Zheyuan (Chinese: 陈哲远; born October 29, 1996), is a Chinese actor and singer. He is best known for his roles in the wuxia drama Handsome Siblings (2020), coming of age Our Secret (2021), romantic fantasy Mr. Bad (2022) and Hidden Love (2023).
